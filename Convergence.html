Our Foundation

Convergence AI was founded in Nashville, Tennessee by three professionals who share a conviction: AI should be deployed with purpose, precision, and an unwavering commitment to doing right by the people it serves.

Methodology

How we deliver
lasting results.

A rigorous, transparent process designed to minimize disruption and maximize the value of every implementation.

1

Discovery & Assessment

We begin by understanding your business at a granular level. Through detailed interviews and process mapping, we identify pain points, bottlenecks, and the highest-value automation opportunities. This phase typically spans one to two weeks and produces a comprehensive implementation roadmap.

2

Solution Architecture

Based on our findings, we design a tailored system that addresses your specific needs. We present detailed specifications, realistic timelines, and expected outcomes so you have complete clarity before a single line of code is written.

3

Secure Development & Testing

Our team builds your custom systems using industry-leading security practices. We conduct rigorous testing in isolated environments to validate reliability and performance before any deployment touches your production systems.

4

Phased Rollout

We deploy in carefully managed phases, minimizing disruption to day-to-day operations. This approach allows us to monitor performance, gather real-world feedback, and make precise adjustments before expanding to full scale.

5

Training & Handoff

Your team receives hands-on training sessions and detailed documentation. We ensure everyone is confident navigating the new systems and understands how to extract maximum value from the tools we have built together.

6

Ongoing Partnership

After launch, we provide continuous monitoring, maintenance, and optimization. Our team remains available for questions, enhancements, and strategic adjustments as your business evolves and new opportunities emerge.

Subscription Model

Your monthly investment covers everything: implementation, training, ongoing support, updates, and continuous optimization. After your initial three-month commitment, you continue month-to-month with complete flexibility.

This model ensures your systems remain current with evolving AI capabilities while providing predictable costs and compounding value over time.

Buyout Option

Following your minimum engagement period, you may elect to purchase full ownership of your custom AI systems at a one-time fee of approximately 8x your monthly rate. This transfers all code, documentation, and intellectual property to your organization.

The buyout option does not include ongoing updates, support, or maintenance. It is designed for businesses with internal technical capacity who seek full control of their systems.
